Order a Print

I’m currently offering prints of the Elvish version of this Map of Middle-earth, either 2 or 3 feet wide and high, on two kinds of paper. Each print is on high-quality paper and individually signed.

The map was illustrated by hand with a nib calligraphy pen. The image itself is a digital composition of scans of individual sections of geography and labels, meticulously translated, transliterated, drawn, scanned, and assembled.

The paper — There are two options for paper: heavy bond paper and vinyl. Vinyl lasts longer and is not readily damaged but costs more. The colors are also more vivid on vinyl.

Variations — The map you receive may be a different version of the map than you will see in the images here or in the version that is posted online. Labels – You will notice that when you zoom in and out of the online version of the map that the labels appear, disappear, and vary in transparency based on their size. In the printed version of the map, all of the labels are visible and vary in transparency in so far as they obscure underlying labels, regardless of their size. Some of the labels are very, very small. Background – The printed version of the map is printed on white paper and relies on the natural texture of the paper for its authentic appearance. The online version is aided by a repeating parchment background to have this effect, but would have diminished the natural authenticity of the printed version. Corrections – I also periodically fix errors, omissions, and replace translations with better ones.

Uniqueness – I personally sign your print and include a version number and an impression number. If you require different dimensions or paper, please contact me at kris.kowal@cixar.com.

Shipping and Handling — It takes about a week to get fresh prints. I keep a pair in stock, but it may take a week and a half for your print to ship from the time you pay.

Returns — I am not presently accepting returns.

— Kris Kowal
